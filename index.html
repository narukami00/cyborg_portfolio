ABOUT CYBORG

KUET CYBORG is the home of all KUETian gamers. Founded at Khulna University of Engineering & Technology in Khulna, Bangladesh, Cyborg unites gamers from across departments under a single banner — to play, compete, and grind together. The club hosts esports competitions within and outside KUET, providing a platform for competitive gaming culture on campus.
